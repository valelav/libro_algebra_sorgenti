Capoverso

Poiché \(g\theta=b_{0}\psi+b_{1}\psi\cdot c+\cdots+b_{m}\psi\cdot c^{m}\) abbiamo
\begin{align*} (f+g)\theta \amp =(a_{0}+b_{0})\psi+(a_{1}+b_{1})\psi\cdot c+\cdots\\ \amp =a_{0}\psi+b_{0}\psi+(a_{1}\psi+b_{1}\psi)\cdot c+\cdots\\ \amp = a_{0}\psi+a_{1}\psi\cdot c+\cdots+b_{0}\psi+b_{1}\psi\cdot c+\cdots \\ \amp = f\theta+g\theta \end{align*}
e
\begin{align*} (fg)\theta \amp =c_{0}\psi + c_{1}\psi c+\cdots\\ \amp = (a_{0}b_{0})\psi+(a_{0}b_{1}+a_{1}b_{0})\psi \cdot c+\cdots \\ \amp = a_{0}\psi\cdot b_{0}\psi +(a_{0}\psi \cdot b_{1}\psi+a_{1}\psi \cdot b_{0}\psi) \cdot c+\cdots\\ \amp =(a_{0}\psi+a_{1}\psi\cdot c+\cdots)(b_{0}\psi+b_{1}\psi\cdot c+\cdots) \\ \amp =f\theta\cdot g\theta\text{.} \end{align*}
Notiamo che abbiamo usato la commutatività di \(S\) nel penultimo passaggio.
