Capoverso

Sia allora \(k\) un intero per cui valga la formula. Abbiamo
\begin{align*} (a+b)^{k+1}\amp =(a+b)^{k}(a+b)=\sum_{i=0}^{k}\binom{k}{i}a^{i}b^{k-i}(a+b)\\ \amp = \sum_{i=0}^{k}\binom{k}{i}a^{i}b^{k-i}a+\sum_{i=0}^{k}\binom{k}{i}a^{i}b^{k-i}b\\ \amp =\sum_{i=0}^{k}\binom{k}{i}a^{i+1}b^{k-i}+\sum_{i=0}^{k}\binom{k}{i}a^{i}b^{k+1-i}\\ \amp =\sum_{i=1}^{k+1}\binom{k}{i-1}a^{i}b^{k+1-i}+\sum_{i=0}^{k}\binom{k}{i}a^{i}b^{k+1-i}\\ \amp=\sum_{i=1}^{k}\left(\binom{k}{i-1}+\binom{k}{i}\right)a^{i}b^{k+1-i}+ a^{k+1}+b^{k+1}\\ \amp =\sum_{i=0}^{k+1}\binom{k+1}{i}a^{i}b^{k+1-i} \end{align*}
dove abbiamo usato la relazione \(\binom{k}{i-1}+\binom{k}{i}=\binom{k+1}{i}\text{.}\)
