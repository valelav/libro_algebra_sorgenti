Capoverso

Infine mostriamo che ogni elemento \((h,n)\) ha come inverso \((h^{-1},n^{-1}\phi_{h^{-1}})\text{.}\) Infatti
\begin{align*} (h,n)(h^{-1},n^{-1}\phi_{h^{-1}}) \amp =(hh^{-1},n\phi_{h^{-1}}\cdot n^{-1}\phi_{h^{-1}})\\ \amp =(1_{H},(nn^{-1})\phi_{h^{-1}})=(1_{H},1_{N}\phi_{h^{-1}})=(1_{H},1_{N}) \end{align*}
e
\begin{align*} (h^{-1},n^{-1}\phi_{h^{-1}})(h,n) \amp =(h^{-1}h,n^{-1}\phi_{h^{-1}}\phi_{h}\cdot n)=(1_{H},n^{-1}\phi_{h^{-1}h}\cdot n)\\ \amp =(1_{H},n^{-1}\phi_{1}\cdot n)=(1_{H},n^{-1}\Id_{N}\cdot n)\\ \amp =(1_{H},n^{-1}n)=(1_{H},1_{N})\text{.} \end{align*}
